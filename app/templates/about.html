Our Story

Agent SDK was founded in 2023 by a team of developers who were frustrated with existing monitoring tools. We believed there had to be a better way to track logs and analyze application performance without the complexity and high costs of traditional solutions.

What started as a simple internal tool quickly grew into a full-fledged platform used by thousands of companies worldwide. Today, Agent SDK processes over 10 million events daily, helping developers build better, more reliable applications.

Our team is distributed across the globe, united by a common mission: to make observability simple, powerful, and accessible to every developer.
